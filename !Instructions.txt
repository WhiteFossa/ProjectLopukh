1) Mirror the part along Z-axis in slicer.

2) Print with very thin walls (2 x layers both vertical and horizontal, layer thickness is 0.3mm).

3) Print from TPU FLEX plastic, use 10% fill, honeycomb pattern.

4) Heat a bit when attaching to headphones.
